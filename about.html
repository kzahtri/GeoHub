Welcome to the Earthquake & Tsunami Educational Hub

The Earthquake & Tsunami Educational Hub (Geo Hub) is a dynamic online platform designed to inform, educate, and empower communities worldwide about natural disasters. By focusing on earthquakes and tsunamis, Geo Hub provides a comprehensive resource for understanding these phenomena, identifying high-risk zones, and enhancing disaster preparedness strategies.

Created as part of a capstone project, this initiative represents the intersection of technology and public awareness, offering cutting-edge tools such as real-time GeoBot assistance and interactive quizzes tailored to boost readiness and knowledge.

Our Core Features

Scientific Foundations

Explore in-depth insights into the science of earthquakes and tsunamis. Gain a deeper understanding of their causes, effects, and the importance of disaster preparedness.

GeoBot Assistant

Our AI-powered assistant is your 24/7 companion for disaster-related queries, delivering accurate, up-to-date information to enhance your awareness.

Interactive Quizzes

Challenge your knowledge with engaging quizzes that test your understanding of disaster scenarios and help you stay prepared for emergencies.
